Calculate your pension

Use this tool to get an estimate of your public sector pension. You can choose between a full pension or a reduced pension, and the tool will show what you may receive monthly and as a gratuity.

The result is an estimate only. Your actual pension depends on records held by the Personnel Administration Division and your last employer.

What you'll need

  • your total months of pensionable service
  • your last annual salary in Barbados dollars

Your Personnel Administration Division and your last employer can confirm both figures.

Before you start

  • No-pay leave does not count towards your pensionable service.
  • Temporary continuous workers with 10 years or fewer of service who leave during that period will not receive a pension.
  • If you were appointed before 15 July 1985, you can retire voluntarily at age 55. If appointed after, the voluntary retirement age is 60.
  • The compulsory retirement age is 67 (from 1 January 2018).
  • If you qualify for both a National Insurance pension and a government pension, only the higher of the two will be paid.
